Our Motivation

We started out as just a group of friends with spare time surfing the interent to find something impactful to do. When we came across the HackPHS event, and were given our prompt, the dangers motorcyclists face on a daily basis stood out to us. This inspired us to create Rello, a software we developed to provide a solution to this problem.
